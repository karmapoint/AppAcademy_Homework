Ingredients

  • 2 cans white beans or garbanzos, drained and rinsed (500g total, once drained)
  • 1 cup quick oats or certified-gf quick oats
  • 1/4 cup applesauce
  • 3 tbsp vegetable or coconut oil
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 and 1/2 cups brown sugar (click for a Sugar Free Cookie Pie)
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Cookie Pie

A delicious and healthy dessert

Description

Do you like eating cookie dough? Who doesn't?!

Then cookie pie might be the perfect dessert for you! It also helps that is low-sugar, has protein from a secret ingrediant, and is even vegan AND gluten-free! This recipe comes from the amazing Chocolate Covered Katie!

Directions

  1. Blend everything (except the chips) very well in a good food processor (not a blender).
  2. Mix in chips, and pour into an oiled 10-inch springform pan.
  3. Cook at 350F for around 35-40 minutes.
  4. Let stand at least 10 minutes before removing from the pan.
